Description

Pizza is an unquestionable corner stone of cousine. One will be hard-pressed to find a corner of the world that has well-established connections to the global stage and is unfamiliar with this oriental dish mastered by the Italians.


Simplistic, in its design, pizza is nonetheless versatile; with a base of bread, cheese, tomato sauce the world around has experimented and created countless variations. Chief amongst which would be the Hawaiian Pizza -- a dish infamous for its controversial reputation for mixing the sweetness of pineapple with the traditionally salty base dish.
